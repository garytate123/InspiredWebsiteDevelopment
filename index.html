HOW WE WORK

CONCEPT

We work closely with my customers to move thoughts & ideas for their websites into a concept. Together we work out WHAT the website is for, WHO it's for & WHY the site is needed. At this stage, we also talk about the functions and features that will be included within their website.

CREATION

After understanding the concept of the website, We start building the website from the ground up. With a good knowledge of HTML, CSS & JavaScript, We are able to build a site this is optimised for SEO, Security & Speed. After writing the code, we work closely with customers to create a site that isn't just designed to look great but to also give the best customer experience possible.

COMPLETION

From the very start of working with customers, we use professional contracts & agree on a Time Frame for the project. Once customers are satisfied with how the website functions & looks, we walk them through each element of their site. All files for the website can either be transferred to customers or we can provide hosting support.

CONTINUATION

After your website goes live and your customers are visiting and using it, I will offer you my maintenance plans. These can be subscribed to or taken up as a one-off. My continuation plans include maintaining, updating, analysing and developing your website.
