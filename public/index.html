HOW WE WORK

CONCEPT

We work closely with our customers to move thoughts & ideas for their websites into a concept.

Together we work out WHAT the website is for, WHO it's for & WHY the site is needed.

At this stage, we also talk about the functions and features that will be included within the website.

CREATION

After understanding the concept of the website, we start building the website from the ground up.
With a good knowledge of HTML, CSS & JavaScript, we are able to build a website that is optimised for SEO, Security & Speed.

After writing the code, we work closely with our customers to create a site that isn't just designed to look great but also gives the best customer user experience possible.

COMPLETION

From the very start of working with customers, we use professional contracts & customers are invited into a project management tool so that they can follow every step of the website build.

Once the project is complete & full payment is made, we deploy the website & transfer all files. We pride ourselves on fantastic customer service from start to finish & offer all our customers follow up support.
